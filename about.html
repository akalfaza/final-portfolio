About Me

Hello, my name is Alara Kalfazade, and I'm a junior studying Computer Science and Economics at Brown University. I received my education from Saint George's Austrian High School in Istanbul, Turkey.

While in high school, I developed a strong interest in business and consulting, which led me to pursue internships in these industries. However, during my studies at Brown, I discovered a passion for UX design in a UI/UX course that I took. The course opened my eyes to the potential of creating innovative solutions that solve real-world problems, and I'm now excited to pursue a career in UX design. I believe that my experience in consulting has provided me with skills in understanding client needs, effective communication, and exposure to a variety of industries, which can be invaluable in creating effective UX designs.

In my free time, I enjoy dancing to house music, playing the piano, watching cooking or true crime shows, and exploring different cultures through travel. I'm currently hooked on Breaking Bad and Better Call Saul, and I'm always looking for recommendations on what to watch or where to travel next.

I'm constantly working to improve my design skills and stay up to date with the latest trends and technologies. I believe that my experiences and interests have equipped me with the necessary skills to excel in the field of UX design, and I'm excited about the potential opportunities that lie ahead.
